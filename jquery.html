Best Tourist Activities

  • Beach Days - Nantucket has beautiful beaches for sunbathing, swimming, and beachcombing, including Surfside and Madaket.
  • Bike Trails - The island accommodates for miles of bike trails, allowing visitors to explore at their own pace.
  • Shopping & Dining - Nantucket's town has unique boutiques, art galleries, and gourmet dining options.

    When to Visit

    While summer is the most popular season, each time of year offers its own charm:



    • Summer (June to August) - Peak tourist season with the warmest weather, ideal for beachgoers and outdoor activities. Book accommodations early, as the island can get busy.
    • Fall (September to October) - Quieter than summer, with mild temperatures and beautiful views. A great time to explore without the crowds.
    • Spring (April to May) - Spring brings blooming flowers and pleasant weather, with fewer tourists.
    • Winter (December to March) - The island has a peaceful, festive feel, with events like the Nantucket Christmas Stroll in early December. Some businesses may close during winter, so plan accordingly.
