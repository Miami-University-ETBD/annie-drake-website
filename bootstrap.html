Birds of Nantucket

Nantucket is home to a diverse array of bird species, including ospreys, piping plovers, and snowy owls.

The island's salt marshes and coastal habitats provide critical nesting and feeding grounds for migratory birds.

  • Seagulls
  • Piping Plovers
  • Ospreys

Marine Life

The surrounding waters of Nantucket are rich with marine life, including seals, dolphins, and whales.

Visitors can often spot harbor seals resting on beaches or humpback whales breaching offshore during seasonal migrations.

Land Mammals and Reptiles

Nantucket hosts a variety of land animals such as eastern cottontail rabbits and white-tailed deer.

Reptiles like painted turtles and garter snakes can also be found in the island's wetlands and grasslands.
